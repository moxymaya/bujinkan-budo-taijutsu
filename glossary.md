4. <a id="boshiken">Boshiken</a>

5. <a id="yugasumi">Yugasumi</a>

9. <a id="toki">Toki</a>

11. <a id="kagato">Kagato</a>

## Fudoken

## Yoko Tobi

Side step

## Nagare

Inch below elbow joint on the forearm.

## Omote

Outside

## Shuto

Striking with the knuckle of the little finger.

## Kasumi

Literal translation: "mist" or "fog"

The **temple** region of the head (the soft spot just above the eye‑socket, near the temporal bone)

A strike here can disorient or temporarily incapacitate an opponent 
because the area is thin‑skinned and sits over the superficial temporal 
artery.

## Kinketsu

Literal translation: "forbidden hole" or "taboo point"

The **sternum** (center of the chest, just below the clavicles)

A powerful blow or thrust to this spot can disrupt breathing, cause 
intense pain, and break the protective “shield” that the rib cage 
normally provides.
