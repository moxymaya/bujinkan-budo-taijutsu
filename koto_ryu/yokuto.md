# Shoden Gata

## Yokuto - scoop up and throw

The meaning of the technique is to throw the opponent sprawling. The important point is to simultaneously pulling your leg back and straight, while also stretching out your left arm fully. You need to nullify Ukes control.

1. Opponent goes to grab [Kumiuchi grip](../glossary.md#kumiuchi).
   
   1. Ideally block their attempt with hand to chest.

2. Thrust up into yugasumi point with the right thumb.

3. [Kakushi Geri](../../glossary.md#kakushi_geri) up into gedan.

4. Left paml strikes up into Gankotsu by going under the arms.

5. Yoko Tobi away
